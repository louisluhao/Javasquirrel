This is an acorn. Collect these and bring them back to your home tree. The acorns in your home tree is your score.
You collect acorns in your cheeks. The more acorns you're holding, the slower you run. Remember to drop them off at home!
There are also special power up items!
Acorn piles fill your cheeks! You can also hit <B> with a mouth full of nuts to drop them into a pile!
Golden acorns make you jump sky high! Hit <G> with a mouth full of nuts to eat them and create a golden acorn!
Coffee! COFFEE! COFFEE COFFEE COFFEE! ZOOM ZOOM! Hit <C> with a mouth full of nuts to eat them and create coffee!
To begin the game, you choose a season.
Burrows can appear from time to time. These are one-way tunnels leading back to your home tree. Stand on a burrow and hit <Spacebar> to use a burrow.
