This is your home. It holds your supply of acorns for the coming winter. The goal is to fill your home with as many acorns as possible.
This is an acorn. Collect these and bring them back to your home tree. You can always see how many acorns are in your home screen in the upper left hand corner.
You collect acorns in your cheeks. You can only hold so many acorns in your cheeks before you must return to your home tree and drop them off. The more acorns you're holding, the slower you run.
There are also special items scattered throughout the forest to power you up!
